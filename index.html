Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

DocuScan Pro is developed by Heinrich Serfontein. This app allows users to scan documents using their device camera and store them locally on their device.

1. Information Collection and Use

DocuScan Pro does not collect, share, or transmit any personal data. All scanned documents and generated PDF files remain on your device unless you choose to share them manually.

2. Permissions Used

  • Camera: Used only to capture document images for scanning.
  • Storage: Used to save scanned documents and PDF files on your device.
  • Internet: Not used to send or upload user data.

3. Data Security

All files remain stored locally. The app does not upload any data to servers or cloud storage.

4. Third-Party Services

DocuScan Pro does not integrate with any external analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected in this section with a new “Last updated” date.
